BATMAN

Vigilante, Dark Knight

Here's a time line of Batman life:

  • 1972 - Bruce Wayne is born into the Wayne family, a wealthy and prominent family in Gotham City. His parents, Thomas and Martha Wayne, are well-respected philanthropists.
  • 1981 - At the age of nine, Bruce witnesses the murder of his parents by a mugger named Joe Chill outside a movie theater.
  • 1990 - Bruce Wayne travels the world, seeking knowledge and training in various disciplines, including martial arts, detective work, and science.
  • 1995 - Bruce trains under masters like Henri Ducard and travels to Nanda Parbat, learning from the League of Shadows.
  • 1998 - Bruce returns to Gotham City, determined to fight crime and corruption.
  • 2000 - He designs the iconic Batman costume and takes on the mantle of the Dark Knight.
  • 2001 - Batman makes his first appearance in Gotham City, striking fear into criminals.
  • 2003 - Batman forms an alliance with Lieutenant James Gordon, who becomes an important ally in his fight against crime.
  • 2005 - He creates the Batcave, a high-tech underground base beneath Wayne Manor, to serve as his headquarters.
  • 2007 - Batman adopts the persona of Bruce Wayne, a billionaire playboy, to maintain a public identity.
  • 2010 - The Bat-Signal is introduced by the Gotham City Police Department to call upon Batman for help.
  • 2013 - Batman faces off against his arch-nemesis, the Joker, for the first time in a battle that will define their eternal rivalry.
  • 2015 - Dick Grayson becomes the first Robin and joins Batman in his crime-fighting endeavors.
  • 2017 - Batman takes in Jason Todd as the second Robin after saving him from a life of crime.
  • 2020 - Tim Drake becomes the third Robin, bringing his intelligence and skills to the Bat-Family.
  • 2022 - Damian Wayne, Bruce's biological son, takes on the role of the fourth Robin.
  • 2025 - Batman becomes a founding member of the Justice League, working alongside other superheroes to face global threats.
  • 2030 - Terry McGinnis becomes the new Batman in the future, under the mentorship of an elderly Bruce Wayne in the "Batman Beyond" series.
